## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit the platform homepage
2. Click **Sign Up** in the navigation
3. Enter your email address and create a password
4. Verify your email address via the confirmation link
5. Complete your profile setup

### Logging In

1. Click **Sign In** in the navigation
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**
4. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

### Password Reset

1. Click **Forgot Password** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for the reset link
4. Create a new password

## GVTEWAY User Guide

GVTEWAY is the consumer-facing platform for discovering events, purchasing tickets, and engaging with the community.

### Discovering Events

#### Browse Events
1. Navigate to **Events** in the main menu
2. Use filters to narrow results:
   - **Category**: Music, Sports, Arts, etc.
   - **Date Range**: Select start and end dates
   - **Location**: Filter by city or venue
   - **Price Range**: Set minimum and maximum prices

#### Search Events
1. Use the search bar at the top of the page
2. Enter event name, artist, or venue
3. Press Enter or click the search icon
4. Results appear instantly with relevance ranking

### Purchasing Tickets

#### Adding to Cart
1. Click on an event to view details
2. Select your ticket type (GA, VIP, etc.)
3. Choose quantity
4. Click **Add to Cart**

#### Checkout Process
1. Review your cart items
2. Apply promo codes if available
3. Click **Proceed to Checkout**
4. Enter payment information
5. Review order summary
6. Click **Complete Purchase**

#### Order Confirmation
- Confirmation email sent immediately
- Tickets available in **My Tickets** section
- Download PDF or add to mobile wallet

### Managing Tickets

#### View Your Tickets
1. Go to **My Tickets** in your profile
2. See all upcoming and past events
3. Click a ticket for details and QR code

#### Transfer Tickets
1. Open the ticket you want to transfer
2. Click **Transfer Ticket**
3. Enter recipient's email address
4. Confirm the transfer
5. Recipient receives email to claim ticket

#### Request Refund
1. Go to **My Orders**
2. Find the order to refund
3. Click **Request Refund**
4. Select reason and submit
5. Refund processed within 5-7 business days

### Profile & Settings

#### Update Profile
1. Go to **Profile** in the menu
2. Edit your information:
   - Display name
   - Profile photo
   - Bio
   - Social links
3. Click **Save Changes**

#### Notification Preferences
1. Go to **Settings** > **Notifications**
2. Toggle preferences:
   - Email notifications
   - Push notifications
   - SMS alerts
   - Marketing communications

#### Payment Methods
1. Go to **Settings** > **Payment Methods**
2. Add new card or bank account
3. Set default payment method
4. Remove outdated methods

### Membership & Rewards

#### Membership Tiers
- **Member**: Free tier with basic benefits
- **Member Plus**: Early access, priority support
- **Member Extra**: VIP pre-sales, exclusive events

#### Earning Points
- Purchase tickets: 1 point per $1
- Write reviews: 50 points
- Refer friends: 500 points per signup
- Attend events: 100 points

#### Redeeming Points
1. Go to **Rewards** in your profile
2. Browse available rewards
3. Select reward and click **Redeem**
4. Points deducted automatically

## ATLVS User Guide

ATLVS is the business operations platform for managing projects, finances, and organizational resources.

### Dashboard Overview

The dashboard provides at-a-glance metrics:
- **Revenue**: Current period revenue vs. target
- **Active Projects**: Projects in progress
- **Outstanding Invoices**: Unpaid invoices
- **Team Utilization**: Resource allocation

### Project Management

#### Creating a Project
1. Go to **Projects** > **New Project**
2. Fill in project details:
   - Project name
   - Client
   - Start and end dates
   - Budget
   - Description
3. Add team members
4. Click **Create Project**

#### Managing Tasks
1. Open a project
2. Go to the **Tasks** tab
3. Click **Add Task**
4. Set task details:
   - Title and description
   - Assignee
   - Due date
   - Priority
5. Track progress with status updates

#### Project Timeline
1. View the **Timeline** tab
2. See milestones and deadlines
3. Drag to adjust dates
4. Dependencies shown with arrows

### Financial Management

#### Creating Invoices
1. Go to **Finance** > **Invoices**
2. Click **New Invoice**
3. Select client and project
4. Add line items:
   - Description
   - Quantity
   - Unit price
5. Set payment terms
6. Click **Send Invoice**

#### Recording Expenses
1. Go to **Finance** > **Expenses**
2. Click **Add Expense**
3. Enter details:
   - Amount
   - Category
   - Vendor
   - Receipt upload
4. Submit for approval if required

#### Budget Tracking
1. Open a project
2. Go to **Budget** tab
3. View:
   - Allocated budget
   - Spent to date
   - Remaining budget
   - Variance alerts

### CRM & Contacts

#### Adding Contacts
1. Go to **CRM** > **Contacts**
2. Click **Add Contact**
3. Enter contact information
4. Associate with organization
5. Add notes and tags

#### Managing Deals
1. Go to **CRM** > **Pipeline**
2. View deals by stage
3. Drag deals between stages
4. Click deal for details
5. Log activities and notes

### Reporting

#### Generating Reports
1. Go to **Reports**
2. Select report type:
   - Financial summary
   - Project status
   - Resource utilization
   - Client analytics
3. Set date range and filters
4. Click **Generate**
5. Export as PDF or Excel

## COMPVSS User Guide

COMPVSS is the production management platform for crew coordination, equipment tracking, and event execution.

### Production Dashboard

The dashboard shows:
- **Active Productions**: Current projects
- **Crew Availability**: Team status
- **Equipment Status**: Inventory overview
- **Upcoming Deadlines**: Critical dates

### Crew Management

#### Viewing Crew Directory
1. Go to **Crew** > **Directory**
2. Browse all crew members
3. Filter by:
   - Skills
   - Availability
   - Rating
   - Department

#### Assigning Crew
1. Open a project
2. Go to **Crew** tab
3. Click **Assign Crew**
4. Search for crew member
5. Select role and dates
6. Confirm assignment

#### Crew Scheduling
1. Go to **Schedule**
2. View calendar by day/week/month
3. See all crew assignments
4. Identify conflicts (highlighted in red)
5. Drag to reassign

### Equipment Management

#### Inventory Overview
1. Go to **Equipment**
2. View all equipment items
3. Filter by:
   - Category
   - Status (available, checked out, maintenance)
   - Location

#### Checking Out Equipment
1. Find the equipment item
2. Click **Check Out**
3. Select project
4. Set expected return date
5. Confirm checkout

#### Returning Equipment
1. Go to **Equipment** > **Checked Out**
2. Find the item
3. Click **Check In**
4. Note condition
5. Report any issues

### Run of Show

#### Creating a Run of Show
1. Open a project
2. Go to **Run of Show** tab
3. Click **Create Run of Show**
4. Add cues:
   - Time
   - Description
   - Department
   - Notes
5. Save and share with team

#### During the Event
1. Open the Run of Show
2. Mark cues as complete
3. Add real-time notes
4. Track actual vs. planned times
5. Log any issues

### Safety & Compliance

#### Reporting Incidents
1. Go to **Safety** > **Incidents**
2. Click **Report Incident**
3. Fill in details:
   - Date and time
   - Location
   - Description
   - Severity
   - Witnesses
4. Submit report
5. Follow up on investigation

#### Certifications
1. Go to **Certifications**
2. View team certifications
3. Track expiration dates
4. Upload new certificates
5. Receive renewal reminders

#### Deactivating Users

1. Go to **Settings** > **Organization**
2. Update:
   - Organization name

   - Contact information
   - Time zone

#### Billing & Subscription
1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**

3. Update payment method
4. View invoice history
5. Upgrade/downgrade plan

## FAQ

### Account & Access

**Q: How do I change my email address?**
A: Go to Settings > Account > Email. Enter your new email and verify it.

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the reset instructions.

**Q: Can I have multiple roles?**
A: Yes, users can have multiple platform roles and event-specific roles.

### Tickets & Orders

**Q: How do I get a refund?**
A: Go to My Orders, find the order, and click "Request Refund." Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

**Q: Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?**
A: Yes, open the ticket and click "Transfer." Enter the recipient's email address.

**Q: My ticket isn't showing up. What should I do?**
A: Check your spam folder for the confirmation email. If still missing, contact support.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The page won't load. What should I do?**
A: Try refreshing the page, clearing your browser cache, or using a different browser.

**Q: I'm getting an error message. What does it mean?**
A: Note the error code and message, then contact support with these details.

### Billing

**Q: How do I update my payment method?**
A: Go to Settings > Payment Methods to add, edit, or remove payment methods.

**Q: Where can I find my invoices?**
A: Go to Settings > Billing > Invoice History to view and download invoices.
